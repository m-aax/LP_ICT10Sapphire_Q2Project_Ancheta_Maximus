About Our Institution

The University of Casterly Rock is a fine education university that was founded in 42 AC by Lann the Clever, the First of the Lannisters. To this day, the University of Casterly Rock is known for its excellence in education, particularly in the fields of finance, politics, and warfare. We are known for producing some of the most influential people in Westeros, including Ser Arthur Dayne, Jaime Lannister, and Tyrion Lannister. This university is funded by the immense wealth of House Lannister, which comes from the gold mines located beneath Casterly Rock, which is what makes this institution one of the most prestigious in all of Westeros. This university offers a wide range of programs and degrees, including: warfare, politics, economics, literature, and history. UCR is a high-quality institution that is dedicated to providing its students with the best education possible.
